## Balanceamento de base.

* Undersampling

* Oversampling

* Método ROSE

### Treinamento do modelo GLM.

